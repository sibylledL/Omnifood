TO THE ROOTS and FASTER

Omnifood-Vege is the Vegetarian counter-part of Omnifood, build thanks a small team of Omnifood staff and independant cooks. If you are busy, don't have time for cooking or think that vegetarian food is always the same and tastless, we are at your disposal.

Remember, all the nutriments come from sun and earth, eating animals your eat a intermediary. Why not look for the origins ?

AS LONG AS THE SUN RISES

Our subscription plans include up to 365 days/year coverage. You can also choose to order more flexibly if that's your style.

DELIVER IN 30 MINUTES

In 30 minutes you will have a consistent and tastful dish deliver to your office or to your home. Dessert and bread can be add to your order.

FARM NETWORK

Thanks to a partnership with local farmers around our hometowns, we are building a social and economical networks adapted to the need of the farmers and our customers. Our cook are working with seasonal vegetables

BALANCED MEAL

Our vegetarian dishes are composed regarding the nature of ingredients to reach the perfect and needed amount of proteins, vitamins, and iron. Our wook work together with nutritionnists in order to offer you balanced and solid composition.
